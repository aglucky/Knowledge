### Circles and Spheres
^scirclesphere
Find description of the points that lie on the intersection of the sphere $x^2+y^2+z^2=36$ and the plane $z=6$

**Steps**
1. Find the center 
   1. Located at $(0,0,0)$
2. Plug plane values into sphere equation
   1. $x^2+y^2+6^2=36$
   2. $x^2+y^2=0$
3. Solve For Possible $x$ and  $y$ equations
   1. $x=0$
   2. $y=0$
4. Answer
   1. $(0,0,6)$
^circlesphere
