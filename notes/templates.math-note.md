## Usage in Mathematics

**Equation 1**:  $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$

**Sample Problem**: 
 What is the hypotenuse of a right triangle with legs of length $3$ and $7$?

**Solution**:
1. Set up the problem
    - $3^2 + 7^2 = c^2$
2. Solve for $c$
    - $c = \sqrt{3^2 + 7^2}=\sqrt{58}$
