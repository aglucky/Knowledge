Right Hand Rule

Definition

Finds the direction of the cross product

Example

Find the direction of a⃗×b⃗\vec{a} \times \vec{b}a×b

Using your right-hand:

  1. point your index finger along a⃗\vec{a}a
  2. point your middle finger along vector a⃗\vec{a}a
  3. a⃗×b⃗\vec{a} \times \vec{b}a×b will be in the direction of your thumb.
